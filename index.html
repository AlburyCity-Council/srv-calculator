Finding your rating category and land value

Both appear on the front page of your most recent rates notice.

  1. Rating category — listed on the first line of the Particulars of Rates & Charges section, for example “Residential” or “Business”. Select the matching category in the calculator.
  2. Land value — shown in the Rateable Value box near the top right. This is the unimproved land value determined by the NSW Valuer General; it does not include your house or other improvements.

Don’t have a rates notice handy? Your land value also appears on the Notice of Valuation from the NSW Valuer General, or you can look it up here.
